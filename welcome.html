FAQ

How do I register for courses?

You can register for courses through the online portal during the registration period.

What is the semester fee?

The semester fee varies by program and includes tuition, library, and lab charges.

How do I access my exam schedule?

Exam schedules are available in the Academics section under "Exam Schedule."

Where can I find my grades?

Grades are posted on the online portal after the examination process is completed.

How do I apply for scholarships?

Visit the Financial Aid section on the website and follow the guided application process.

What support services are available?

We offer counseling, career advice, and academic support services through our student center.

How do I contact the administration?

Use the "Contact Us" section to get in touch with the university administration.

What are the library hours?

The library is open from 8am to 10pm on weekdays and 9am to 6pm on weekends.

How can I access campus Wi-Fi?

Campus Wi-Fi credentials are provided during your orientation and can be found on the IT portal.

Where can I find academic calendars?

Academic calendars are published under the Academics section and updated regularly.

How do I submit assignments online?

Assignments can be submitted through the student portal under the "Assignments" tab.

Who do I contact for technical support?

For technical issues, reach out to the IT helpdesk via email or the contact form provided online.
